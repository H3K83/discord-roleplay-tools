Welcome to Discord Roleplay Tools!
As you might’ve guessed, Discord Roleplay Tools is a completely free collection of text-based roleplaying tools that help might make roleplay on Discord servers easier. These tools range in variety and will be added to as thought of or by necessity, but we welcome you to browse our lineup as it is now. We have a lot of great tools that we’ve put a tremendous amount of passion into and we are positive that you’ll be able to get something out of them. If not, that’s okay, just be sure to check back every now and again as this site grows, evolves and begins to add more tools. Otherwise, feel free to throw any polite commentary our way via the GitHub link that can be found down in the copyright section of the site.

Thank you so much for coming and we hope these tools help and that you’ll enjoy them!

-Hekate
Profile Card Generator
The Profile Card Generator is a simple tool to create a Discord-like profile card, take a snapshot of it and use it to head off roleplay posts.
Stylized Post Generator
If you hate character limits and can't afford Discord Nitro, this tool is your friend. Enter in the post you plan to make, capture a snapshot of it and post — it's that simple!
